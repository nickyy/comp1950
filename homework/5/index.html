General Description

Looking for that perfect getaway home, but just not finding it within a stones throw? Wanting something a little out of the ordinary, with a splash of character, and maybe with a bit of history? Look no further than this beautiful, sun-drenched and spacious, uniquely hand-crafted, one of a kind abode, fit for a KING! Once the tallest man-made structure at 139 metres high, this home, at one point the living space to ancient kings and queens from the days of yore, boasts a rich (and ancient) history. It was painstakingly crafted by no fewer than 20,000 men over a duration of 20 years, weighs 5.9 million tons, and is made of approximately 2.3 million limestone blocks! Needless to say, blood, sweat, and tears brought this massively marvelous masterpiece into existence ... and maybe by some decree of an eccentric Egyptian pharaoh named Khufu.

Adorning various passageways, corridors, and rooms, are intricately carved heiroglyphics, still not fully decipherable to man, but the longer you spend in this new home of yours, the more you'll probably learn! Included with the splendor and majesty of this exquisite edifice is a subterranean, unfinished chamber which can be used as your bomb shelter.

This palatial estate is also a mathematical marvel, at no extra cost to you: with a perimeter to height ratio of 1760/280 cubits, this equates to 2π to an accuracy better than 0.05% (corresponding to the well-known approximation of π as 22/7). Even the four corners of its base correspond almost exactly to the four cardinal points!
